FIXED GEAR FOREVER

16 APRIL 2011

The veloheld combines minimalist design with superb quailty. Devoid of excessive graphics and gear shift components,the veloheld product range delights us with its beauty and simplicity. The black and white(yin and yang?) bicycles feature a short wheelbase, a single gear and a narrow handlebar... all you need explore the city streets.
